3.1 Legal basis

Where the PDPA applies, we rely on your consent, or on exceptions permitted under the Act including performance of a contract and legitimate interests.

Where the GDPR applies, we rely on:

  • Contract — to provide the Service you have signed up for
  • Legitimate interests — security, abuse prevention, and service improvement
  • Legal obligation — tax, accounting, and regulatory record keeping
  • Consent — where separately requested, such as marketing emails

3.2 Marketing

We will only send marketing emails if you opt in. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in the email or by writing to info@usino.ai. Service-related messages, such as billing notices, are not marketing and cannot be opted out of while you hold an account.

We protect personal data using measures including:

No system is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we will notify you and the Personal Data Protection Commission of any breach where required by law.

10. Your rights under the PDPA

You may:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you

To exercise these rights, email info@usino.ai. We will respond within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity first.

Withdrawing consent may mean we can no longer provide the Service.
